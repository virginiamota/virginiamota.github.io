About

I'm a professor at the Informatics Department of COLTEC - Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG), Brazil.

Always working to encourage girls to enter the STEM area and to promote awareness on accessibility, I have supervised works that won the Technovation Challenge (Mappid, 2020), Change the Game (2nd Edition), and Gamethons (2023).
